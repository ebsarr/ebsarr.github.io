About

January 8, 2017 · 1

Hello Everyone! Thank you all for landing in my blog.

The idea of creating a blog has been on my mind for a very long time. I’ve resisted it for many reasons, but mainly because I’m naturally shy, don’t like to talk about myself or my opinions, or even what I’m thinking, especially when nobody is asking. That is what bloging is about right? Talking about stuffs that people might not even care about. I however would like to get better at writing and expressing myself in general. That is why I decided to create this blog.

Here is an overview of the things I’m intersted in and might write about:

  • IT, mainly cloud computing
  • Coding: I write code in python, golang and some shell scripting
  • Politics, I guess I’ll most of the time keep my political opinions to myself

From time to time, I might also write about some really unrelated subject, like sports, movies, music, whatever comes to my mind you know…

I hope you’ll enjoy this blog. Thank you!!!

- EbSarr
