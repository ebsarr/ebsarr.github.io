Painful love

2017/05/10
  • poem
  • Tweet

i love you so much, it hurts sometimes
i think so much about you, i forget to breath sometimes
i am so full of love for you, but feel so lonely sometimes

i feel disoriented, lost and alone in this world
i want you so bad, to bring you to my world
i want so bad our worlds to merge

help me, help me please, help me be better
help me babe become sane and clean
help me regain balance in my miserable life

i am so messed up without you
i am so fucking lost, so out of purpose
my soul is closer and closer to nothing without you

i am in need of your reciprocal love
that i know i can’t have but can’t let go
where should i head to babe, i don’t know

i don’t even feel stress or physical pain
i don’t even feel the cold of winter, neither the heat of summer
i don’t even feel thirsty, neither hungry

all i feel is love
all i feel is loneliness
all i feel is pain

I cry all the time,
it’s funny but i cry absent tears
just internal bleeding from my heart, it feels like death is near

help me babe,
help me, let me guide you into my world my love
help me, save me please from this painful love
